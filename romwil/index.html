2024 is bringing new trends to the fitness world, including a focus on functional fitness and gamified workouts. The trend toward functional fitness, where exercises mimic real-life movements, is becoming more popular, with gyms offering specialized equipment like battle ropes and tire flips. Meanwhile, gamification is taking off, with fitness equipment now integrating gaming experiences to keep members engaged while working out. Additionally, wellness is taking center stage, with more people using fitness as a way to improve overall health, not just aesthetics"
